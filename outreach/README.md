## ðŸ“§ Email Best Practices

### Sending Schedule

**Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
**Best time:** 8-10 AM CET (before inbox gets full)
**Avoid:** Monday (too busy), Friday (thinking about weekend)

### Follow-Up Schedule

**First follow-up:** 7 days after initial email
**Second follow-up:** 14 days after first follow-up
**Third follow-up:** Don't send a third. Move on.

### Email Account

**Send from:** info@taxed.ch (professional email)
**Avoid:** Personal Gmail/Hotmail (looks unprofessional)
**Signature:** Include full contact info, website link

### Personalization Tips

**Do:**
- Reference specific recent articles from their site
- Use editor's name (research it!)
- Mention why your content fits their audience
- Keep it concise (under 200 words)

**Don't:**
- Send generic "Dear Editor" emails
- Use overly salesy language
- Send attachments in first email (unless requested)
- Send on weekends

## ðŸŽ¯ Common Questions

### Q: What if I don't get responses?

**A:** This is normal! Industry average response rates:
- Guest posts: 20-30%
- Resource pages: 15-25%
- Press releases: 5-10% pickup

Don't be discouraged. It's a numbers game. Keep sending.

---

### Q: How long until I see backlinks?

**A:** Timeline varies:
- Press release syndication: 1-7 days (fastest)
- Guest posts: 2-6 weeks (after acceptance)
- University resource pages: 2-8 weeks (slow bureaucracy)
- Partnerships: 1-3 months (relationship building)

---

### Q: What if my pitch is rejected?

**A:**
1. Thank them for considering
2. Ask for feedback (what would make it a better fit?)
3. Offer alternative topics
4. Keep them on your list for future pitches
5. Move on to next target

---

### Q: Should I offer to pay for guest posts?

**A:** No! Offers like these are:
- Guest posts: Always free (you provide value via content)
- Sponsored posts: Clearly disclosed, different process
- Never offer money unsolicited (can hurt reputation)

---

### Q: How do I know if a backlink is working?

**A:**
1. Check in Google Search Console (Sitemaps > Discovered via)
2. Use Ahrefs to monitor new backlinks
3. Track referral traffic in Google Analytics
4. Look for Domain Authority increase (monthly check)

## ðŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Issue: Email bounces / invalid address

**Fix:**
- Use Hunter.io to find correct email
- Check publication's "Contact" or "About" page
- Look for author bylines with email
- Try LinkedIn InMail as alternative

---

### Issue: No responses after 2 weeks

**Fix:**
- Send polite follow-up (template included)
- Try different contact (e.g., different editor)
- Adjust pitch angle based on recent content
- Move on if still no response after follow-up

---

### Issue: Editor says "not a good fit"

**Fix:**
- Ask: "What topics would be a good fit?"
- Offer 2-3 alternative article ideas
- Thank them and add note to tracking sheet
- Revisit in 2-3 months with different angle

---

### Issue: Guest post accepted but not published

**Fix:**
- Follow up politely: "Just checking on publication timeline"
- Offer to update/revise if needed
- Be patient (can take 2-6 weeks)
- If >8 weeks, ask if they still plan to publish
